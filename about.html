Hey, welcome to JTRTechnology

Who's behind this site?

I'm an IT professional based in the Austin, TX area who spends way too much time researching tech — and decided to make that habit useful for other people. JTRTechnology is where I share honest recommendations on the gear and tools I actually use and believe in.

No fluff, no filler. Just straightforward takes from someone who works in tech every day.

My interests cover a pretty wide range of tech, which means so do my recommendations:

Why trust my recommendations?

I buy and use the things I write about. I'm not regurgitating spec sheets or Amazon listings — I'm sharing what's actually worked for me and what hasn't. When something isn't worth your money, I'll say so.

I also have a day job in IT, so I have a decent eye for separating marketing hype from real-world performance.
